INSIGHTS SUMMARY

1. 🔄 Rolling Sales Trends:
   - Q4 months consistently outperform others by 15-25%.
   - Notable dip in Q2 suggests a seasonal slump or missed promotions.

2. 📊 Customer Segmentation:
   - 18% of customers are 'High Value', contributing over 50% of sales.
   - Targeting them with loyalty campaigns could significantly boost retention.

3. 💸 Discount Dynamics:
   - Discounts above 20% are associated with negative average profit.
   - Optimal range for discounting appears to be 5–10%.

4. 🌍 Regional Performance:
   - West and East lead in sales, but Central yields highest profit margins.
   - Office Supplies underperform across all regions except West.
